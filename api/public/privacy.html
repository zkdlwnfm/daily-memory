Privacy Policy

Effective: April 7, 2026

Engram ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal memory companion that helps you record and organize your daily experiences. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address (if you sign in via email, Apple, or Google)
  • Memories: Text content, photos, extracted entities (people, places, amounts) that you create
  • Usage data: API call counts, feature usage (no content is logged)
  • Device data: Device type, OS version (via Firebase Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide AI analysis of your memories (via OpenAI)
  • To enable cloud sync across your devices
  • To send you reminders and notifications
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Data Storage

  • Your memories are stored locally on your device (CoreData) and optionally synced to Firebase Cloud (Firestore)
  • Photos are stored locally and optionally backed up to Firebase Storage
  • Vector embeddings for search are stored on our secure servers
  • All data is isolated per user - no one else can access your memories

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, cloud sync, analytics
  • OpenAI: AI text analysis, image analysis, embeddings (your content is sent to OpenAI for processing but not stored by them)

5. Data Retention

Your data is kept as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, export, or delete your data at any time
  • Use the app without an account (local-only mode)
  • Opt out of AI analysis
  • Lock individual memories for extra privacy

7. Children's Privacy

Engram is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
