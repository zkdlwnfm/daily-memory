Features
From capture to recall,
every memory managed
AI structures your daily records and semantic search finds the memory you need, whenever you need it.
🎙️

Voice & Text Capture

Real-time speech recognition converts your voice to text instantly. Type when you prefer — switch freely between modes.

🧠

AI Auto-Analysis

Automatically extracts people, places, amounts, promises, and emotions from every memory. Categories assigned instantly.

🔍

Semantic Search

Ask in natural language — "What did I promise Sarah last month?" — and find memories by meaning, not just keywords.

👥

People Tracking

Auto-tracks mentioned people with relationship types, meeting frequency, and last contact date.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Time and location-based reminders. Geofencing triggers alerts when you arrive at a place.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Offline-first design. Memories sync automatically when you're back online with conflict resolution built in.

    Sentiment analysis — Tracks mood scores and visualizes trends
  • 🖼️
    Image analysis — Recognizes objects, scenes, and text (OCR) in photos
  • 🔗
    Vector embeddings — text-embedding-3-small for semantic matching
