## Description
Engram is your personal AI memory companion. Record daily moments with voice or text, and let AI automatically extract the people, places, events, and even your mood.

KEY FEATURES:

- Voice & Text Recording
Speak naturally or type. Real-time speech recognition captures every word.

- AI-Powered Analysis
AI automatically extracts people, locations, dates, financial amounts, and mood from your entries. No manual tagging needed.

- Semantic Search
Ask natural questions like "When did I last see Sarah?" or "What promises have I made?" and get intelligent answers.

- AI Chat
Have a conversation with your memories. Ask follow-up questions and explore your personal history.

- People Tracking
Automatically builds your relationship network. Get alerts when you haven't contacted someone in a while.

- Smart Reminders
Time-based and location-based reminders. AI detects promises and events to remind you automatically.

- Mood Tracking
See how your emotions change over time with AI-detected mood trends.

- Cloud Sync
Your memories sync across devices. Offline-first — works without internet.

- Privacy First
Biometric lock, per-memory encryption, and local-only mode available.

PREMIUM FEATURES:
- 200 AI analyses per day (vs 30 free)
- 100 image analyses per day (vs 10 free)
- 500 semantic searches per day (vs 50 free)
- Advanced weekly & monthly AI reports
- Priority cloud sync

Your memories matter. Engram makes sure you never forget.
